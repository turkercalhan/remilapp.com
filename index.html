Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Welcome to Remil ("we", "our", or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Remil mobile application ("App"). By using Remil, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you register via email, Google Sign-In, or Apple Sign-In.
  • Photos You Upload: Images of coffee cups or palms that you voluntarily submit for AI analysis. These images are transmitted to Google Gemini API for processing and are not stored on our servers beyond the duration of the session.
  • Reading History: Your fortune-telling and astrology readings are stored in your personal account on Firebase Firestore to power your journal feature.
  • Usage Data: Anonymous app usage analytics (feature interactions, session duration) collected via Firebase Analytics.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and app version for crash reporting and performance monitoring via Firebase Crashlytics.
  • Push Notification Token: If you allow notifications, we store your device token to send personalized reminders.
  • Purchase Information: Subscription status managed via RevenueCat. We do not store full payment details — all billing is handled by Apple.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To deliver personalized AI-generated fortune-telling and astrology readings.
  • To maintain your reading journal and account history.
  • To send optional push notifications (daily horoscopes, streaks, reminders).
  • To process in-app purchases and manage subscription access.
  • To improve app performance and fix crashes.
  • To show relevant advertisements (if you have not subscribed to Premium).
Important: We never sell your personal data to third parties. Photos you submit for reading are used solely for AI analysis and are not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose.

3. Third-Party Services

Remil integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their own privacy policies:

  • Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics, Cloud Messaging) — Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini AI (Image and text analysis) — Google AI Terms
  • Google Sign-In — Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Sign-In — Apple Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat (Subscription management) — RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Google AdMob (Advertising for non-premium users) — Google Privacy Policy

4. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at privacy@remilapp.com. Account deletion is also available within the app under Profile → Settings → Delete Account.

5. Children's Privacy

Remil is not intended for children under the age of 17. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and Firebase security rules to restrict data access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights including:

  • Access to the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction of inaccurate data.
  • Deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • Opt-out of personalized advertising (via iOS Settings → Privacy → Tracking).
  • Data portability.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@remilapp.com.

8. Tracking & Advertising (ATT)

Remil may request permission to track your activity across apps and websites (App Tracking Transparency) to serve relevant advertisements. This permission is entirely optional — you can use the app and decline tracking without losing core functionality. Premium subscribers do not see advertisements.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via a notice in the app or by email. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
